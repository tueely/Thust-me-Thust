About

Here is a timeline of Thust me Thust

  • Initiation

    In the Aga Khan Academy Mombasa, the Thust me Thust idea sparked between residents from Tajikistan, their goal was to recieve donations and use them in assisting people who are affected by the pandemic in Tajikistan as they're locked in School

  • Teaming up

    Teams were made based on people's specialities and this helped increase efficieny on making this goal a reality and helping people in Tajikistan who are currently facing such issues (which includes the relatives of the people in Aga Khan)

  • Transition

    The website was coded, the GoFundMe page was initiated and many social media accounts relating to Thust me Thust were brought up as well, through this, the organization was becoming a better place and the right goal for Tajikistan

  • Donation

    Now, we're waiting for a donation (may be your donation?)
